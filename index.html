What we do

At Northwest Firepits, we love making your backyard special with our firepits! It's not just about getting a firepit, but enjoying cozy nights, roasting marshmallows, telling stories, and making memories. Let's light up the night and make every moment shine!

Who we are

Northwest Firepits isn't just a business. It's about three best friends who love hanging out together. We started because we loved telling stories and laughing around our own firepit. Now, we want to help make your backyard a fun and cozy place for your family and friends. Let's light up the fun, one yard at a time!
